Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 May 2026 · Effective: 9 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SICHER MAYOR COMMERCIAL BROKERS L.L.C ("BeastPay", "we", "us", "our"), a company licensed by the Dubai Department of Economic Development (DED Licence No. 841208) and registered at Dubai, United Arab Emirates, collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the BeastPay payment gateway (the "Service") at sichermayor.online and associated APIs.

By using the Service you consent to the practices described below. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

1. Who is the data controller?

SICHER MAYOR COMMERCIAL BROKERS L.L.C is the data controller for personal information processed through the Service. Contact: privacy@sichermayor.online.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Identity & contact: name, date of birth, nationality, residential address, email, phone number.
  • KYC documents: government-issued ID, proof of address, selfie/liveness imagery, source-of-funds attestations — collected only when a transaction exceeds the relevant KYC threshold.
  • Merchant onboarding data: trade licence, certificate of incorporation, MOA/AOA, beneficial-owner details, bank statements.
  • Wallet addresses you supply for receiving cryptocurrency.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • IP address, device identifiers, browser type, operating system, language.
  • Transaction metadata: amount, currency, timestamps, provider used, success/failure outcomes.
  • Cookies and similar technologies for session integrity and fraud prevention.

2.3 Information from third parties

  • KYC/AML providers (e.g. Sumsub) verifying identity documents you submit.
  • Payment processors confirming transaction outcomes.
  • Sanctions and PEP screening services.
What we do NOT collect: we do not store full primary account numbers (PANs), CVV/CVC values, or PIN codes. Card data is tokenised at the processor; BeastPay only retains last-4, brand, BIN, expiry, and a non-reversible fingerprint.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and secure the Service and process your transactions.
  • Comply with applicable anti-money-laundering ("AML"), counter-terrorist-financing ("CTF"), and sanctions laws — including UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 20 of 2018 and Cabinet Decision No. 10 of 2019.
  • Verify your identity through tiered KYC checks (see §4).
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access.
  • Send service notifications (transaction alerts, security alerts).
  • Respond to lawful requests from regulators, courts, and law-enforcement authorities.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service.

4. KYC tiers and thresholds

BeastPay applies risk-based KYC proportional to transaction value:

TierThresholdInformation collected
Tier 0Below the configured free limitWallet address, email (optional)

Tier 2Above the email-KYC limitFull identity verification via Sumsub: ID document, liveness, address proof, source-of-funds

5. Legal bases for processing

  • Contract: processing necessary to perform the Service you requested.
  • Legal obligation: AML/CTF, sanctions, tax, and licensing requirements.
  • Legitimate interest: fraud prevention, system integrity, network security.
  • Consent: where required for marketing communications (which you may withdraw at any time).

6. Sharing your information

We share information only with parties necessary to operate the Service:

  • Payment & on-ramp providers (e.g. Stripe, Transak, MoonPay, Ziina, CoinRemitter, Plisio, NOWPayments) to execute the transaction you initiate.
  • KYC/AML providers (Sumsub and equivalents) for identity verification and sanctions screening.
  • Cloud-infrastructure providers (Google Cloud Platform — hosting in us-central1) for compute, storage, and network services.
  • Communications providers (Telegram, WhatsApp/Meta, email providers) where you opted in to receive alerts.
  • Professional advisers (auditors, legal counsel) under confidentiality.
  • Regulators and law enforcement when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

We do not sell personal information.

7. International transfers

Some processors and infrastructure are located outside the United Arab Emirates (notably the United States and the European Economic Area). When personal information is transferred outside the UAE, we rely on adequate-level recipients, contractual safeguards, or your consent. By using the Service you acknowledge such transfers.

8. Data retention

  • KYC records and transaction logs: retained for a minimum of five (5) years after the last transaction, in compliance with UAE AML/CTF regulations.
  • Account data: retained while your account is active and for 5 years thereafter.
  • Marketing preferences: retained until you withdraw consent.
  • Server access logs: typically retained for 90 days.

9. Security

We apply technical and organisational measures including TLS in transit, AES-256-GCM encryption of sensitive credentials at rest, role-based access control, key rotation, and continuous logging. No internet-facing system is perfectly secure, and you remain responsible for protecting your own credentials and wallet keys.

10. Your rights

Subject to UAE law and applicable foreign law (where relevant), you may:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure, where retention is not required by law.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Lodge a complaint with the UAE Data Office or another competent authority.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@sichermayor.online. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

11. Children

The Service is not directed at, and may not be used by, persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

12. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for session integrity and security. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through the Service.
